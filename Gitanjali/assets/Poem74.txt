The day is no more, the shadow is upon the earth. It is time that I go to the stream to fill my pitcher.<br/><br/>

The evening air is eager with the sad music of the water. Ah, it calls me out into the dusk. In the lonely lane there is no passer-by, the wind is up, the ripples are rampant in the river.<br/><br/>

I know not if I shall come back home. I know not whom I shall chance to meet. There at the fording in the little boat the unknown man plays upon his lute.<br/><br/>
