He came and sat by my side but I woke not. What a cursed sleep it was, O miserable me!<br/><br/>

He came when the night was still; he had his harp in his hands, and my dreams became resonant with its melodies. <br/><br/>

Alas, why are my nights all thus lost? Ah, why do I ever miss his sight whose breath touches my sleep?<br/><br/>
