 I thought that my voyage had come to its end at the last limit of my power, -- that the path before me was closed, that provisions were exhausted and the time come to take shelter in a silent obscurity.<br/><br/>

But I find that thy will knows no end in me.<br/> And when old words die out on the tongue, new melodies break forth from the heart; and where the old tracks are lost, new country is revealed with its wonders.<br/><br/>
